THE SAN FRANCISCO 10x PARTY

We throw parties and do projects to make the best city in the world 10x better.

This city has a lot of problems, but even more potential. Momentum is on the side of San Francisco turning around and becoming a truly great city. It just needs a little push. We want to help by gathering together energetic people who love this city and love building things into a great party in all senses of the word.

OUR FIVE THESES:

  1. San Francisco is under-developed, especially housing and transportation.

  2. Our children should be able to walk barefoot on any street in the city.

  3. The SF government could be small, meritocratic, and magnificent.

  4. Families should move to San Francisco for the public schools, not away.

  5. San Francisco would be at the center of America's industrial renaissance.

OUR CURRENT PROJECTS:
